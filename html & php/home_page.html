About us

MME:Money Made Easy is an e-banking service. In this website, activities start with MME automating a customer account, which allows personnel to create, update and maintain customer records. The MME website have enhanced the accuracy of accounts that tellers and other banking personnel process. This website performs customer transactions through a centralized data record system. Account management is the genesis and backbone of all banking information systems.
